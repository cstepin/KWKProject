Programs

Some great programs for learning more about coding include:

Kode With Klossy

A great program for female and non-binary students to learn an introduction into web development or app design. People of all programming backgrounds welcome! (13+ y.o.)

Code With Google

A program where students of traditionally disenfranchised backgrounds have the opportunity to learn code. Students enter as beginner coders and are welcome to join at any age (starting with 9+ y.o.)

Scholarships

We want you to continue learning without worrying about financial costs! Check out a couple resources below to support future coding ventures.

Science Ambassador Scholarship

Scholarship open for any female high school senior or undergraduate college student planning to study STEM. Submit a video explaining any STEM topic.

Women at Microsoft Scholarship

Scholarship open for any female or non-binary high school senior planning to study STEAM at an accredited 2-4 year university. Submit essays, letter of recommendations, and transcript.

Snapology STEM Studies Scholarship

Scholarship open for any female high school senior planning to study STEAM at an accredited 2-4 year university. Submit an essay.

CollegeVine Scholarships

Variety of scholarships available. Check site for more information.
