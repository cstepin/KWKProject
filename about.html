About Us:

Glow and Grow est. 2021

Our Mission:

As a team, we are dedicated to creating Glow and Grow, a website that aims to provide easily accessible resources for underrepresented students in STEM.

Meet the founder:

Christina Stepin is an 18-year-old innovator with a passion for social justice. Having had only one computer science class offered at her own school, Christina was inspired to ensure that other girls interested in computer science could pursue the subject to the fullest extent. Therefore, Christina joined the Glow and Grow team. In her free time, Christina enjoys baking, biking, and entertaining her two cats.
